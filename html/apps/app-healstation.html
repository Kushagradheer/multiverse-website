HealStation Organization

SEE THE PROJECT
Description

When the Nonprofit HealStation organization came to us with their challenges and their aim to solve the problem of Mental health issues and their undertreatment, we wanted to work on this project and wanted to be a part of HealStation's great initiatives.

The problem was simple-
In rural India, there are multiple villages covered by an NGO, but these villages can have hundreds and thousands of people, and all of their screening was to be done by multiple MHWs (Mental Health Workers).

These workers traditionally would walk door to door with a clipboard of thousands of copies of same questionnaire, asking the same questions to every member of each house. The total number of people to get screened would vary from thousands to hundreds of thousands!

Apart from screening, the organizations also had to manage the teams, monthly events, coordinate between different clusters of villages, and most of all, handle the paperwork in a very systematic way.

In rural India, there are multiple villages covered by an NGO, but these villages can have hundreds and thousands of people, and all of their screening was to be done by multiple MHWs (Mental Health Workers).

These MHWs will be managed by a Team Leader, whose separate app will be given.

One all the people in a cluster (a group of villages) are screened, a Doctor confirms the symptoms of all the probable patients and supplies their medicine. We have to develop a separate Doctor's app for this.

All the past patient data would be represented in a timeline, so any probable symptoms, medications and family and patient's info can be viewed at all times.

For the organization itself, the main objective for them is to view all the data that matters and see an aggregated view to get to know the real impact they're actually making.

We supplied an Organization mobile and web app for this.
